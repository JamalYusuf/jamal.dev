I am a technology leader, architect, researcher, and lifelong student of complex systems. My work sits at the intersection of enterprise architecture and the science of human expertise — studying how the best minds perceive, decide, and build, then engineering platforms that put those patterns into production.

For over fifteen years I have designed and operated mission-critical distributed systems in highly regulated healthcare, telecom, and privacy-sensitive environments — Go backends, Kafka pipelines, and reliability engineering at enterprise scale.

More recently, I have led organization-wide Generative AI adoption: production agents, governed data platforms on Databricks and Snowflake, and developer enablement programs — with observability, compliance, and long-term maintainability as non-negotiables.
