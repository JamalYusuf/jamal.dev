This Privacy Policy describes how **Jamal Yusuf** ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit [Jamal.dev](https://jamal.dev/) (the "Site"). This Site is operated from the United States and is intended for a general audience.

## Information I Collect

### Information you provide

If you contact me by email or through LinkedIn, I receive the information you choose to send (such as your name, email address, and message content). I use that information only to respond and maintain a professional correspondence record as needed.

### Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, standard server and browser data may be recorded, such as:

- IP address and approximate location derived from it
- Browser type, device type, operating system, and language
- Pages visited, referring URL, and timestamps
- Technical data needed to deliver the Site securely and reliably

I do not operate a user account system on this Site and do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information through forms on the Site itself.

### Third-party services

The Site loads resources from third-party providers that may collect information according to their own policies, including:

- **Tailwind CSS CDN** (script delivery)
- **Font Awesome CDN** (icon fonts and styles)
- **LinkedIn and GitHub** (when you follow outbound links)

If you click external links, those sites are governed by their own privacy practices.

## How I Use Information

I use information to:

- Operate, maintain, and improve the Site
- Understand aggregate traffic and performance
- Respond to inquiries and professional requests
- Protect the Site against abuse, fraud, or security issues
- Comply with applicable law

I do not sell your personal information.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Site may use browser local storage for preferences (such as theme selection) and may rely on third-party CDNs that set cookies or similar identifiers for delivery, security, or performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

## Legal Bases and U.S. State Privacy Rights

If you are a resident of California or another U.S. state with a consumer privacy law, you may have rights to know, access, correct, delete, or opt out of certain processing of personal information, subject to legal exceptions.

Because this Site does not sell personal information and does not use personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, there is typically no opt-out of sale/sharing to register. You may still contact me to request access or deletion of personal information you provided directly.

I will not discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights.

## Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact me and I will take appropriate steps to delete it.

## Data Retention

I retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law or legitimate business needs (for example, responding to an inquiry or maintaining basic security logs).

## Security

I use reasonable administrative and technical measures appropriate to a personal professional website. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## International Visitors

If you access the Site from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

## Changes to This Policy
