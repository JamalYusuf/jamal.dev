Jamal Yusuf is committed to making [Jamal.dev](https://jamal.dev/) accessible to people with disabilities. I aim to conform to widely recognized accessibility practices, including the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA** where reasonably practicable for a personal professional website.

## Measures Taken

Accessibility considerations on the Site include:

- Semantic HTML structure and heading hierarchy on primary templates
- Keyboard-accessible navigation and interactive controls where supported
- Visible focus states on links and buttons
- Text alternatives for meaningful images where implemented
- Sufficient color contrast in core light and dark themes
- Responsive layouts for different screen sizes and zoom levels
- Respect for `prefers-reduced-motion` where motion effects are used

## Known Limitations

Some areas may present accessibility challenges, including:

- Third-party assets loaded from external CDNs (for example, icon fonts)
- Legacy or embedded content that may not fully meet preferred contrast or structure targets
- PDF or print-oriented resume views generated by the browser
- Occasional experimental UI features under active development

I continue to improve accessibility as the Site evolves.

## Feedback and Assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of the Site, or wish to request content in an alternative format, please contact me with:

- The page URL
- A description of the barrier encountered
- The assistive technology or browser used, if applicable

I will make reasonable efforts to respond and address issues promptly.
